## 1. One-Person Business Model

### Core Philosophy
- Entrepreneurship is modern survival. The industrial age model of specializing in one career for 40 years is dying
- AI will replace highly specialized, repetitive tasks. The entry-level is going extinct
- You don't find a niche — **you ARE the niche**. Build a business around your life, curiosities, and problems
- The internet created a decentralized economy where self-improvers and polymaths thrive

### Key Principles
- **Permissionless Leverage**: Media (content) and code (software) allow one person to replicate efforts infinitely at zero marginal cost
- **Distribution = Freedom**: Your audience is your distribution. A mediocre product with massive distribution beats an incredible product with zero distribution
- **Traffic + Offers**: The fundamental equation — capture attention (traffic) and direct it toward a valuable solution (an offer)
- **Evolve or Die (Koe's Law)**: Work evolves to earn more in the time allotted. Freelancer → Group consulting → Scalable digital products

### Frameworks

#### The Value Equation
Value = Problem + Goal + Process + People

#### The 4 Pillars (Macronutrients of Business)
1. **Brand** — Your identity, story, and the vision you lead people toward
2. **Content** — The map that helps people overcome roadblocks
3. **Product** — Your systemized solution to solve problems faster
4. **Marketing/Promotions** — The benefits and "why they should care"

#### The Entrepreneurial Method
1. Find a problem in your own life
2. Learn how to solve it
3. Document how you solved it online
4. Systemize the process
5. Share your discoveries for profit

#### Build, Teach, Earn
- Build skills and projects
- Teach what you learn to build an audience
- Earn by monetizing your aggregated knowledge

#### The Development-Based Path
- Pursue the "eternal markets" (Health, Wealth, Relationships, Happiness)
- Teach what you learn, help your past self achieve goals faster

#### Post-Labor Economics
- Transition from labor wages to capital income
- Your unique value = owning assets (brand, audience, digital products) + your specific perspective

### Actionable Strategies
- **The 4-Tool Digital Stack**: 1) Social media for free traffic, 2) Newsletter (Beehiiv) to own audience, 3) Storefront (Stan Store), 4) Idea system (Cortex/Notion)
- **Start with Micro-Service**: Sell 4-call coaching package for $1,000 or an e-book/template before building big products
- **Pre-Sell Before You Build**: Landing page → Persuasive copy → Sell the idea → Let payments create deadline
- **The 1,000 True Fans Model**: Small loyal audience buying $150 course / $25 subscription / $5,000 coaching

## 3. Writing & Content Creation

### Core Philosophy
- Writing is the foundation of ALL media and the greatest skill of the 21st century
- In an AI-flooded world, **taste, perspective, and curation** are the ultimate moats
- Content is idea warfare on mental real estate — plant perspective-shifting ideas
- Social media is a minimum viable product testing ground

### Key Principles
- **Curation Over Creation**: People follow you for YOUR specific lens, not raw information
- **Time Under Attention**: Long-form content builds deep trust far outweighing short-form dopamine hits
- **Originality is a Myth**: Original thinking = exploring existing ideas in fresh ways by connecting concepts from different domains
- **Write One Idea 1,000 Ways**: Take 5-10 core philosophies and rewrite using different hooks, stories, structures
- **Broad to Specific Funnel**: Top-of-funnel (social) = broad/beginner-friendly. Back-end (newsletter/products) = niche/advanced

### Frameworks

#### The 2-Hour Content Ecosystem
1. Write one 1,000-word newsletter per week
2. Break actionable points into a Twitter/X thread
3. Break thread into 3 daily short-form posts
4. Cross-post across all platforms

#### The APAG Framework
- **A**ttention — compelling hook/headline
- **P**erspective — amplify the problem and enemy
- **A**dvantage — your new, better vision
- **G**amify — step-by-step actionable advice

#### The PPP Framework (Short-Form)
- **P**ull — catch attention
- **P**erspective — unique angle
- **P**unchline — hard-hitting conclusion

#### The Pyramid Principle
Start with main conclusion → Prove with 3+ arguments → Support with facts/data/anecdotes

#### PASTOR (Long-Form Copywriting)
Problem → Amplify → Story → Testimony → Offer → Response

#### C.O.R.E. Notes / Problem & Process
Attach a Problem (attention) → Goal → Example → Benefit → Process (step-by-step) → Concept (catchy name)

#### The Topic Tree
"You" at top → 3 broad interests → Sub-topics → Hyper-specific actionable ideas

#### The 10 Commandments of Engagement
Specific numbers, pattern interrupts, negativity bias, target call-outs, problem call-outs, potential benefits, social proof, absolute conviction, punchy formatting, cautionary warnings

### Actionable Strategies
- **Build an Idea Museum (Swipe File)**: Save 20-30 high-engagement posts, break down structure, inject your ideas into proven frameworks
- **Scroll as a Researcher**: Stop consuming, start analyzing what works and why
